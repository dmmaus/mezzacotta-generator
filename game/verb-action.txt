# Generic words for actions you might perform with physical game components
#
# ~:    I draw a card
# _S:   She draws the card
# _ED:  He drew the card
# _P:   The card was drawn by a player
# _ING: He is drawing a card
#
@format ~|S|ED|P|ING
choose|chooses|chose|chosen|choosing
display|displays|displayed|displayed|displaying
draw|draws|drew|drawn|drawing
select|selects|selected|selected|selecting
discard|discards|discarded|discarded|discarding
meld|melds|melded|melded|melding
control|controls|controlled|controlled|controlling
