# Generic words for ways of "paying" some commodity
#
# ~:    I draw a card
# _S:   She draws the card
# _ED:  He drew the card
# _P:   The card was drawn by a player
# _ING: He is drawing a card
#
@format ~|S|ED|P|ING
donate|donates|donated|donated|donating
exchange|exchanges|exchanged|exchanged|exchanging
pay|pays|paid|paid|paying
